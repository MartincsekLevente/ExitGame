Dark forces surrounded your city. Their latest goal is to prevent the completion of the beautiful cathedral under construction. Under the cover of the night, a thief crept into the abbey to steal the blueprints. You and your fellow monks need to recover the stolen blueprints to prevent scandal. You have 60 minutes to overcome all obstacles and get out. Solving the tasks requires real teamwork and common thinking. Use your intelligence and go beyond the minds of those who make traps!
