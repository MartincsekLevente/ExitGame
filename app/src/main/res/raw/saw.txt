Someone came up with a crazy game against you: Hello! I want to play a game! You've already done a lot of things in your life that make you a prisoner now. You have 60 minutes to escape before the toxic gas enters the room and reaches the deadly level. The rules are simple: solve the puzzles. and open the padlocks! You can find the last key that will get you out of the room. When the time runs out, the poison will kill you.
